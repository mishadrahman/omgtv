Safety Guidelines

Welcome to OMG TV! To keep our community safe, fun, and respectful for everyone, please adhere to these safety guidelines when using our platform.

1. Keep Your Personal Information Private

Never share your real name, address, phone number, social media profiles, or financial information with strangers. You are fully anonymous on OMG TV; stay that way to protect yourself from scams or harassment.

2. No Nudity or Sexual Content

OMG TV strictly prohibits nudity, sexually explicit content, and sexual propositioning. Users found broadcasting explicit behavior will be permanently banned.

3. Be Respectful to Others

  • No hate speech, racism, or discrimination based on gender, religion, or sexual orientation.
  • No bullying, threats, or harassment of other users.
  • If you don't like who you are matched with, simply press the "Next" button. Be polite and move on.

4. Age Requirements

You must be 18 years or older to use OMG TV. We do not permit minors to use the service.

5. Don't Fall for Scams

Be wary of links sent by strangers. Do not click random URLs, and never send money or cryptos to anyone you meet on the platform.

6. Report Bad Behavior

If you encounter someone breaking the rules, threatening you, or making you uncomfortable, please use the in-app reporting feature before disconnecting. Reporting helps us ban disruptive users from the platform.


By using OMG TV, you agree to these safety guidelines and our terms of service.
