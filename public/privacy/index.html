Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

OMG TV operates as an anonymous chat platform. We do not require registration or collect personal identifiable information (PII) like your name, email, or phone number to use our core services.

2. Anonymous Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the performance and quality of our webRTC video delivery and text chat matching systems. This includes device type, browser information, and regional data to match you efficiently.

3. Video and Audio Data

OMG TV uses peer-to-peer (P2P) WebRTC connections where possible. In P2P mode, the video and audio streams travel directly between you and the stranger. We do not record or store your video or audio conversations on our servers.

4. Chat Logs

Text chats are stored temporarily in order to deliver them to the connected partner. Once a session ends, these logs are routinely cleared. They are not stored permanently.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party analytics to track website performance and traffic. These services obey standard privacy regulations and utilize aggregated, non-personally identifiable metrics.

6. User Safety & Reporting

While we do not store chat logs permanently, reported sessions or users violating our Terms and Safety Guidelines may have basic metadata flagged temporarily to enforce bans and keep the community safe.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
